Features

🏃

Motion Detection

Triggers theft mode the moment your Mac is picked up, tilted, or carried away — using the built-in accelerometer. Responds instantly while ignoring everyday bumps.

🔊

Siren Alarm

Loud synthesized alarm to draw attention

☕

Sleep Prevention

Keeps your Mac awake so tracking continues

👆

Touch ID Disarm

Quickly disarm with biometric authentication — one tap to return to normal mode.

🔔

Telegram Alerts

Instant notifications to your personal Telegram bot with location, IP, Wi-Fi, and battery info — updated every 20 seconds.

🔒

Lock Screen

Display a custom message on the stolen device

📍

Real-time Tracking

GPS location, IP address, Wi-Fi network, battery

📱

Bluetooth Auto-Arm

Arms when your phone leaves Bluetooth range

⌨️

Global Hotkey

Arm and disarm with a keyboard shortcut

📡

Remote Control via Telegram

Control your armed Mac from anywhere — stop the alarm, check device status, disarm protection, all through Telegram commands.

Requirements

  • ● Apple Silicon Mac (M1 Pro, M2, or newer)
  • ● macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later
  • ● Telegram bot (free to create)
  • ● Optional: LidGuard Helper for motion detection, lock screen, sleep prevention, and power button alerts

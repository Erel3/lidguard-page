LidGuard

Laptop theft protection for macOS

LidGuard protects your MacBook from theft. When armed, it monitors lid close and power disconnect events, then instantly sends your device's location, IP address, Wi-Fi network, and battery level to your Telegram bot.

Features

  • Lid close and power disconnect detection
  • Real-time Telegram alerts with device tracking
  • GPS location updates every 20 seconds
  • Loud siren alarm
  • Bluetooth auto-arm when your phone leaves range
  • Sleep prevention during theft mode
  • Touch ID disarm and remote control via Telegram
  • Lock screen overlay on stolen device
  • Global keyboard shortcut

Requirements

  • macOS 14.0 or later
  • Telegram bot (free to create)
  • Optional: LidGuard Helper for advanced features
